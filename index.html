Founded by a gallerist, art historian and creative coder, clad & co. is an art-based firm specializing in making your website more accessible to you. Our goal is to help the uninitiated coder feel more comfortable and confident in updating and dealing with their website.

Making a change to your website shouldn't be overly complicated. If you've ever wanted to make a visual or small text-based change to your homepage, like uploading an image or putting up your most recent news, then look no further. We're here every step of the way from implementation, to tutorials, to answering any all questions that may come up.

clad & co. have developed a simple add-on for your existing website that allows you to update your site your way. And the best part is you can try it here.

Our team has over 5 years of professional art-world experience, and a whopping 30 years of coding behind them so you're in safe hands.
